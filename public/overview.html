Key Features

100% On-Chain Gameplay

Every farming action is recorded and enforced by smart contracts. No off-chain manipulation possible - true fairness for all players.

Play-to-Earn Mechanics

Earn Rice Tokens (RT) through harvesting crops, completing quests, and daily rewards. Convert to ERC-20 tokens for real value.

Seamless Experience

In-game wallet system eliminates annoying signing pop-ups. Enjoy instant transactions with Shreds integration on Rise testnet.

Deep Strategy

Manage 16-plot farm with energy system, tools, and upgrades. Strategic planning required for optimal yields and profits.

Decentralized Marketplace

Purchase seeds, tools, and bundles with ETH or RT. All transactions are transparent and supply-limited for value creation.

Community Driven

Global leaderboards, daily quests, achievements, and player governance. Join a thriving community of blockchain farmers.

Global Farm

Interact with the community farm, see other players' progress, and participate in collaborative farming activities.

Leaderboard

Compete with farmers worldwide, track your ranking, and climb the global leaderboards for recognition and rewards.

Inventory System

Manage your collected items, tools, and resources. Track your progress and organize your farming equipment efficiently.
